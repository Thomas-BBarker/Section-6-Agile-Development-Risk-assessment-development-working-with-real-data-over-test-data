Monitoring and Detecting Corporate Data Breaches

When companies suffer security breaches, hackers dump millions of user credentials onto dark web forums. The primary risk here is automated "credential stuffing" attacks, where hackers use software bots to systematically test your leaked email and password combinations across retail, banking, and streaming platforms. Proactive security means treating your accounts as compromised until proven otherwise. You should leverage monitoring services to receive instant alerts when your data is detected in a breach, allowing you to change exposed passwords before malicious access occurs.
